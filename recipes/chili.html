Basic Chili

The following recipe is a modified version of You Suck at cooking recipe. Chili is a dish that is very easy to make your own as it is endless customizable. There is no wrong way to make chili, but this is a good way to start.

Ingredients

  • 2 Pounds Ground Meat (Beef, Turkey, Chicken, etc...)
  • 1 pound Onion (Yellow or White)
  • 1 Green Bell Pepper
  • 4 Cloves of Garlic
  • 1 Tsbp Olive Oil
  • 28oz Crushed Tomatoes
  • 2 Tsbp of Chili Powder
  • 1 Tsbp of Ground Cumin
  • 1-3 Tsp Kosher Salt (Adjust to taste)
  • 1 Tsp of Black Pepper
  • 1-2 Cans of Beans (Kidney, Black, etc...)
  • 1 Cup of Water
