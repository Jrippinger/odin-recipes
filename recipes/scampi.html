Shrimp Scampi

Shrimp Scampi is a simple pasta recipe, it is a great recipe to learn the basics of cooking.

Ingredients

  • Shrimp
  • Spaghetti
  • Garlic
  • Butter
  • Olive oil
  • Lemon
  • Salt
  • Black pepper
  • Red Chili Flakes

Directions

  1. Bring pot to boil and add salt and pasta
  2. On meduim heat in a pan add olive oil, once shimmering add uncooked shrimp to pan. Cook evenly on both sides
  3. Set aside cooked shrimp and add butter and garlic to the pan
  4. Once the garlic is lightly cooked add Spices to salt to the butter sauce to taste
  5. Add pasta and shrimp to pan. Toss around in sauce to get everything well coated
  6. Serve in bowl with juice from a lemon wedge
